## The challenge

TxtCart's affiliate program was growing, but their existing solution couldn't scale with it. Three roadblocks kept showing up:

- A rigid affiliate structure with no variable commission options
- No way to customize the affiliate experience without engineering work
- Manual payment processes that ate into the team's week

<Quote author="Kyle Bigley" role="CEO and Founder, TxtCart">
We went the first three years with no affiliate program, mainly because there was no clean solution for Shopify in particular. Since switching to Mantle for our affiliate program, we've nearly doubled our affiliates, generated 791 referrals, $131K in referral revenue, and paid out $25K in commissions.
</Quote>

## The solution

Mantle Affiliates replaced a stack of point tools with a single program TxtCart could actually shape. The pieces that mattered:

### Flexible group-based commissions

Variable rates for different affiliate tiers. No spreadsheets, no custom code — every commission rule lives in one place.

### Streamlined affiliate onboarding

Branded registration pages built inside Mantle, so TxtCart's affiliates land somewhere that feels like home without engineering involvement.

### Expanded affiliate network

The Affiliate Marketplace connected TxtCart with potential partners, driving the bulk of their network growth.

### Global payment infrastructure

Stripe-powered payouts across 60+ countries with automated processing — no more chasing PayPal links or manual transfers.

### Flexible program management

Manual overrides and per-relationship assignments mean TxtCart can adjust in real time when a partner needs something different.

## The outcome

A program that pays for itself, runs itself, and grows on its own.

- **Program growth.** Affiliate count doubled from ~100 to 192, with 791 tracked referrals.
- **Revenue growth.** $131k in referral revenue and $25k paid out in commissions.
- **Time savings.** 2–4 hours back every month, plus a fully automated payout pipeline.

Mantle is a great centralised tool for CX, email marketing, and affiliate management. The financial features are my favourite part.
</Quote>

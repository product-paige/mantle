## Overview

This template helps app teams create better Shopify App Store visuals without starting from scratch. It packages the layout patterns, copy formulas, and AI-assisted generation steps used by Mantle apps that consistently win the merchant comprehension test.

You won't need to design from a blank canvas. Each section gives you a structured starting point you can adapt to your app's category, audience, and visual brand — while preserving the conversion patterns that work on the Shopify App Store.

## How it works

Provide one of:

- Your app listing URL
- Your landing page
- Existing screenshots
- A feature list

The workflow turns that input into structured **screen concepts**, **headline copy**, and **layout directions** — ready to hand to a designer, paste into a generation tool, or screenshot directly from your product.

## What you can create

- **Bento-style app screens** — multi-feature compositions in one frame
- **Mobile mockup screens** — phone-frame product shots with overlay copy
- **Feature spotlight screens** — single-feature deep-dive screens
- **Workflow explanation screens** — step-by-step "how it works" panels
- **Conversion-focused headline variations** — A/B-ready merchant-language headlines

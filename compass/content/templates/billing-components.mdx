Reusable Polaris-compatible billing and subscription components for Shopify apps built with Mantle.

Built for teams that want to move faster without rebuilding common SaaS billing patterns from scratch. Production-ready components for pricing, subscriptions, upgrades, usage states, customer billing, and plan management.

---

## What it includes

- Pricing components
- Subscription management UI
- Plan comparison layouts
- Usage-based billing states
- Upgrade and downgrade flows
- Empty, loading, and error states
- Polaris-compatible components
- Mantle-connected billing patterns

## How it works

The components are designed to plug directly into Mantle-powered apps and Shopify workflows.

Use them as:

- production-ready billing UI
- pricing page starters
- reusable SaaS foundations
- customizable Polaris patterns

Most components connect directly with Mantle plans, subscriptions, customers, billing actions, and usage data.

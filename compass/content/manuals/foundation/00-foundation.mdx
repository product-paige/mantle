Most founders skip this manual. They open a builder, start prompting, and figure out the business side later. That works until it doesn't, usually around the time they realize they've built something nobody's paying for.

Foundation is the chapter you read before you touch a tool. It covers what AI actually changed about building software, who this guide is for, whether this can be a real business, and the mindset shift that separates builders who ship from builders who stay in permanent beta.

It's the shortest manual in Compass. Read it once and you'll move faster through everything that follows.

## Chapters

- **0.1 What AI changed** — The gap between idea and working product collapsed. Here's what that means and what it didn't fix.
- **0.2 Who this is for** — For founders who can build but are figuring out the business side for the first time. Web, mobile, AI tools, or traditional code.
- **0.3 Can this be a real business?** — Yes. The constraint was never technical sophistication, it was the cost of getting from idea to product in front of real users. That cost is gone.
- **0.4 You're a founder now** — Calling yourself a vibe coder gives you a built-in excuse not to think about the business. Here's why that's a trap.
- **0.5 How to use this guide** — Seven manuals, 33 chapters, one through-line: everything you need to go from idea to operating business.

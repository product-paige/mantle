Before you open a builder, you need to know what you're building, who it's for, and whether it's worth building at all. That's what this manual is for.

Most vibe coders skip straight to Build. Some of them get lucky. Most spend six weeks on something nobody wanted, quietly archive the repo, and start the next thing. The Shape manual is the six hours that prevents the six weeks.

## Description

Use Mantle AI to turn scattered knowledge into one clear source of truth for your team.

This framework helps you generate internal training docs for products, features, workflows, or policies so everyone explains things the same way and works faster.

Less tribal knowledge. Fewer repeat questions. Better handoffs.

## Best for

<ul className="framework-checklist">
  <li>onboarding new hires</li>
  <li>support enablement</li>
  <li>sales training</li>
  <li>success handoff docs</li>
  <li>internal SOPs</li>
  <li>feature launch education</li>
  <li>policy/process documentation</li>
</ul>

## Use with

- **Mantle AI** inside your dashboard
- **Internal team enablement** and operations

## How to use

Enter the topic you need documented:

- **Product / feature / process / policy** — what needs to be explained clearly
- **Audience** — new hires, support, sales, success, ops, cross-functional team
- **Purpose** — train the team, reduce confusion, improve consistency, speed handoffs

The output should give you:

- what it is
- why it matters
- who it's for
- definitions
- how it works
- workflows
- common scenarios
- recommended language
- FAQs
- edge cases
- escalation guidance

## What to look for

<ul className="framework-checklist">
  <li>fast readability</li>
  <li>clear ownership</li>
  <li>consistent terminology</li>
  <li>practical next steps</li>
  <li>fewer grey areas</li>
  <li>reusable team language</li>
</ul>

## Why it works

Most internal docs fail because they are vague, outdated, or written for the author.

Strong training docs help the next person do the job correctly without needing a meeting.

## Reminder

If knowledge lives only in Slack threads and people's heads, it is expensive.

Document once. Use often.

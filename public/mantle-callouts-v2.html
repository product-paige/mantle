002 Shape · Reality Check

Reality Check

Building an app and building a business aren't the same job. The first is engineering; the second is figuring out who actually pays, and why they'd pay again.

003 Build · Common Failure

Common Failure

Founders launch with short trials because they want faster conversion numbers, but the actual setup takes longer than the trial itself. The users who would have paid never get far enough to see the value.

Reframe formula — dark
Framework
The attention shift
Instead of asking...
Ask...
"How do I increase activation?"
→
"What does someone need to feel after their first session to come back?"
"What features should I add?"
→
"What is the next thing my best customer is hoping I'll fix?"
"Why are people churning?"
→
"What's the moment my product disappointed them?"
These are the same questions in some sense. But the framing changes what you go looking for — and what you go looking for usually changes what you find.

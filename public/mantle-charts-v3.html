Framework
Three laws of AI-visible content
The signals AI search rewards. Build your content strategy around these.
01
Information gain
Every piece must say something that doesn't exist elsewhere. Synthesis, original data, or a unique perspective.
02
Human signal
Named authors, demonstrable expertise, and third-party citations. AI surfaces content it can attribute.
03
High-velocity freshness
Updated within 90 days. AI search weights recency heavily. A publishing cadence is a competitive moat.
